about paul

Paul Harlyn is a producer, piano player, synthesist, sound designer, studio owner, and arranger. But his specialty is working with artists and singer-songwriters. From concept to the finished master, Paul helps the artists he works with to bring out their musical vision.

As an artist, Paul has released three nationally distributed world music CD's under the project name Great Barrier. The song “Cairo” off of his first CD was released on Buddha Bar Vol. 2 (Buddha Bar Records) that has had sales of over 450,000 units.

Paul now resides in central Florida after working in NYC for 15 years.
